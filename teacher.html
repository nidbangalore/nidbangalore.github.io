Discipline Co-Lead, Digital Game Design Head, Physical Computing Lab (Bengaluru Campus)

Anoop Saxena

An Artist, Designer and Educator, with specialization in Science Communication and Electronics. Anoop holds comprehensive and multifaceted professional experience in New Media Design, Robotics, STEM, wearable technology, exhibition design and curation. Currently, he is serving at the National Institute of Design, Bangalore as an Associate faculty in the discipline of Digital Game Design. His work has been exhibited in national and International Art Festival.
